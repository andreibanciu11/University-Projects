Short description

Real Madrid is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain. It was founded in 1902 and is one of the most successful football clubs in history, having won numerous domestic and international titles, including 13 UEFA Champions League trophies. Real Madrid plays its home matches at the Santiago Bernabéu[1] Stadium and has a large and passionate fan base around the world. The club has a rich history, with many legendary players and coaches having represented the team over the years, and is known for its commitment to excellence and high level of performance on the field.

He is widely regarded one of the most important figures in the history of Real Madrid, having served as its president for 35 years, from 11 September 1943 until his death in 1978.
